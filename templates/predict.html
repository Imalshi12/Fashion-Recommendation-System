Measurement Instructions

Please follow these simple steps to accurately take your body measurements before filling out the form below.

  • Dress Size: Enter your usual dress size, the size you typically wear.
  • Breasts (cm): Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure horizontal.
  • Waist (cm): Find the narrowest part of your waist, just above your belly button, and measure around this area.
  • Hips (cm): Measure around the widest part of your hips, ensuring the tape stays horizontal.
  • Shoe Size: Enter the size you usually wear for shoes.
  • Height (cm): Measure your height in centimeters while standing straight against a wall.
  • Weight (kg): Weigh yourself using a scale and enter your weight in kilograms.
